What you need for a raise

Below is the list of things investors usually expect to see before they commit. We’ve put it together so you know what to prepare.

Checklist
01

Story

A short version of why this product, why now, and why you. Used in the deck and in every conversation.

02

Investor deck

10–14 slides covering the company, product, market, team, and the ask. The first thing every investor opens.

03

A product investors can try

Mockups in the deck plus a clickable demo so investors can see what Galgal looks and feels like.

04

Fundraise plan & use of funds

A breakdown of how the money will be spent in the first 12 months: team, build, growth, and runway, tied to milestones. We prepare it with you. It becomes a slide in the deck.

05

Team on the slide

You plus product & tech advisors named on the team slide.

Optional

Idea validation · sign-up page

A landing page where Jewish women can sign up to join Galgal before launch. Lets you walk into investor meetings with real numbers instead of just an idea.
